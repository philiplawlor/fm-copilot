# FM Copilot - Go-to-Market Strategy

## Executive Summary

FM Copilot's GTM strategy focuses on building credibility in the facilities management industry through authentic, helpful engagement while demonstrating measurable ROI. We target facility managers and maintenance teams frustrated with traditional CMMS limitations, positioning FM Copilot as a practical AI copilot that delivers immediate value.

## Market Opportunity

### Total Addressable Market

**Facilities Management Market Size:**
- Global facilities management market: $1.2 trillion (2024)
- CMMS software market: $5.2 billion (2024)
- AI in facilities management: $2.1 billion projected (2028)

**Serviceable Addressable Market:**
- Target: Small to medium facilities (1-50 buildings)
- Focus: Organizations with 5-200 maintenance staff
- Initial markets: US, Canada, UK, Australia

### Competitive Landscape

**Direct Competitors:**
- CMMS vendors adding AI features (Fiix, UpKeep, ServiceChannel)
- Predictive maintenance startups (Augury, 3DEXPERIENCE)
- Workflow automation tools (ServiceNow, IBM Maximo)

**Differentiation:**
- Platform-agnostic (works with any CMMS)
- Focus on daily workflow friction vs. dashboard analytics
- Designed for imperfect data from day one
- Faster time-to-value (weeks vs months)

## Target Customer Profile

### Primary Persona: Facility Manager

**Demographics:**
- Age: 35-55
- Title: Facilities Manager, Operations Director, Maintenance Manager
- Organization size: 50-500 employees
- Facility portfolio: 5-50 buildings
- Annual budget: $500K-$5M facilities spend

**Pain Points:**
- 40% of time spent on administrative work
- Manual work order triage and assignment
- Reactive maintenance causing budget overruns
- Poor handover from construction to operations
- Skepticism about "AI-powered" marketing claims

**Buying Triggers:**
- Demonstrated 40% time savings in pilot
- Integration with existing CMMS without replacement
- Works with current messy data
- Clear ROI within 90 days

### Secondary Persona: Maintenance Technician

**Demographics:**
- Age: 25-50
- Title: Maintenance Technician, Facilities Technician
- Experience: 5-20 years in facilities
- Mobile-first users

**Pain Points:**
- Time wasted on paperwork and data entry
- Unclear work order instructions
- Manual scheduling and coordination
- Limited access to asset history

## Pricing Strategy

**Positioning:**
- **Starter ($500/month):** Entry point for small teams
- **Professional ($1,500/month):** Core market for medium facilities

**Value Anchoring:**
- Competitor analysis shows 3-5x higher pricing for similar capabilities

- Emphasize quick ROI and time savings

**Trial-to-Paid Conversion:**

## Risk Mitigation

### Market Risks

**AI Skepticism:**
- Solution: Focus on practical, measurable outcomes
- Demonstrate value through free trials and pilots
- Share customer success stories and metrics

**Competition:**
- Solution: Differentiate through platform-agnostic approach
- Focus on workflow integration vs. feature checklists
- Build strong community presence and credibility

### Execution Risks

**Sales Cycle Length:**
- Solution: Streamline demo and trial process
- Provide self-service evaluation tools
- Focus on quick wins and measurable ROI

**Integration Complexity:**
- Solution: Simplify integration requirements
- Provide CSV import/export as fallback
- Develop integration partners and marketplace
