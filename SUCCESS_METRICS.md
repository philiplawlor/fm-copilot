# FM Copilot - Success Metrics & KPIs

## Executive Summary

FM Copilot measures success through a comprehensive framework of quantitative metrics and qualitative indicators. Our success metrics focus on three core areas: product performance, customer value, and business sustainability.

## Primary Success Metrics

### Time Savings (40% Administrative Time Reduction)

**Measurement Method:**
- Pre-implementation baseline surveys
- Post-implementation time tracking
- User-reported time diaries
- Work order processing analytics

**Target Metrics:**
- **Work Order Creation**: Reduce from 8-10 minutes to under 2 minutes (75% reduction)
- **PM Schedule Setup**: Reduce from 45 minutes to under 5 minutes (90% reduction)
- **Weekly Administrative Time**: 10+ hours saved per facility manager

**Tracking Method:**
- In-app time tracking features
- User surveys (monthly)
- Analytics dashboard with automated reporting

### AI Accuracy & Adoption

**Measurement Method:**
- AI recommendation acceptance rates
- Work order auto-categorization accuracy
- Technician dispatch recommendation accuracy
- PM template suggestion relevance

**Target Metrics:**
- **AI Acceptance Rate**: >75% of recommendations accepted without modification
- **Work Order Accuracy**: >80% of requests correctly parsed and categorized
- **Dispatch Accuracy**: >85% of technician assignments accepted
- **PM Template Usage**: >90% of templates accepted with minor modifications

**Tracking Method:**
- Database logging of AI interactions
- User feedback mechanisms
- A/B testing of AI improvements

### User Engagement & Adoption

**Measurement Method:**
- Daily/weekly active users
- Feature usage analytics
- Session duration and frequency
- Work order processing volumes

**Target Metrics:**
- **Daily Active Users**: >70% of licensed users
- **Feature Adoption**: >80% of users using AI features weekly
- **Session Duration**: Average 15+ minutes per session
- **Work Order Volume**: 2x increase in processed work orders

**Tracking Method:**
- Application analytics (Mixpanel/Segment)
- Database usage logs
- User engagement surveys

## Secondary Success Metrics

### Customer Satisfaction

**Net Promoter Score (NPS):**
- Target: >70 (Industry leading)
- Measurement: Quarterly surveys
- Tracking: Promoter/detractor analysis

**Customer Satisfaction (CSAT):**
- Target: >4.5/5.0
- Measurement: Post-interaction surveys
- Tracking: Support ticket resolutions, feature requests

### Operational Efficiency

**Platform Performance:**
- **Uptime**: 99.9% availability
- **Response Time**: <500ms API responses (95th percentile)
- **Error Rate**: <0.1% API error rate

**Scalability Metrics:**
- **Concurrent Users**: Support for 10,000+ concurrent users
- **Data Processing**: Handle 100,000+ work orders monthly
- **AI Processing**: <2 second response time for AI requests

### Business Sustainability

**Financial Metrics:**
- **MRR Growth**: 20% month-over-month growth
- **Customer Acquisition Cost**: <$500 per customer
- **Customer Lifetime Value**: >$10,000 per customer
- **Churn Rate**: <5% annual churn

**Unit Economics:**
- **Payback Period**: <6 months for enterprise customers
- **ROI**: 300% within 12 months
- **Gross Margins**: >80% after customer acquisition costs

## Risk Indicators & Mitigation

### Early Warning Signals

**Product Risks:**
- Declining feature adoption rates
- Increasing error rates or performance issues
- Negative user feedback trends
- Competitive feature releases

**Business Risks:**
- Slowing growth rates
- Increasing churn indicators
- Rising customer acquisition costs
- Market share erosion

**Operational Risks:**
- Infrastructure scaling issues
- Security incidents or breaches
- Compliance violations
- Team productivity declines

### Mitigation Strategies

**Product Issues:**
- Regular user testing and feedback sessions
- Proactive feature monitoring and optimization
- Competitive analysis and feature gap identification
- Customer advisory board engagement

**Business Issues:**
- Diversified customer acquisition channels
- Proactive churn prevention programs
- Competitive pricing and feature analysis
- Market research and trend analysis

**Operational Issues:**
- Infrastructure monitoring and auto-scaling
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- Compliance monitoring and reporting
- Team training and development programs
